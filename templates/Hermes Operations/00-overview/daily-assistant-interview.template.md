# Daily Assistant Interview Template

Use this in private Operations to discover useful Hermes workflows.

Do not store real answers in a public repo.

## Interview prompt

```text
I want you to interview me and figure out where you can help me most.

Ask me questions about:
- my work;
- recurring tasks;
- recurring decisions;
- messages;
- research;
- content creation;
- admin;
- calendar;
- files;
- tasks I avoid;
- things I keep checking manually.

Ask one question at a time.

After each answer, identify possible workflows you could take over or help with.

At the end, give me:
1. The top ten tasks you can help with.
2. The three easiest workflows we can test today.
3. The three highest-leverage workflows we should consider this week.
4. The tools, files, permissions, or context needed for each.
5. A short daily check-in prompt.

Do not suggest vague ideas. Only suggest workflows based on what I actually tell you.
Classify risky workflows as approval-only or manual.
```

## Output fields

- Interview date: `<YYYY-MM-DD>`
- Interview owner: `<PERSON_OR_ROLE>`
- Main responsibilities: `<SUMMARY>`
- Recurring tasks: `<LIST>`
- Repeated decisions: `<LIST>`
- Repeated messages: `<LIST>`
- Research needs: `<LIST>`
- Content/admin/calendar/file needs: `<LIST>`
- Avoided tasks: `<LIST>`
- Candidate workflows: `<LIST>`
- Required tools/context/permissions: `<LIST>`
- Safety notes: `<NOTES>`
